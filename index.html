THE LANTERN IN THE STORM

CHARACTERS:
Eli - A young boy who loves the sea and shows courage.
Villagers - People who become afraid when the storm begins.
Sailors - Men at sea who need light to reach home safely.

SETTING:
The story happens in a small village near the ocean and a lighthouse on a stormy night.

CONFLICT:
The lighthouse lamp suddenly goes out during the storm. Ships are in danger, and Eli must face his fear to bring light back.

MORAL LESSON:
Even a small act of bravery can save lives and create great change.
